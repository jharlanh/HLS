Certified Translations

These are translations which  carry the official seal (legal validation) and are most commonly used for embassies, courts of law, foreign affairs, university–related matters, hearings, legal procedures, etc.

There are many common items that require certification, such as: marriage and birth certificates, company charters, articles of incorporation, academic report cards, letters of recommendation, contracts, death certificates, passports, visas and other types of consular services.
