About Us


Managed and operated by a multilingual team from the United States, Great Britain and Mexico, Horbach Language Services has provided a complete range of language services throughout the United States, Canada, Europe and Latin America (especially Mexico) for more than 30 years.




HLS provides personalized, confidential, and speedy services which focus on the specific needs of each client. As a result of this approach, our client portfolio includes over half of the Fortune 500 Corporations, numerous medium and small size businesses as well as embassies and major financial institutions.
