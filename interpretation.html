Simultaneous & Consecutive Interpretation


In addition to our high-quality translations, we also offer simultaneous interpreting services for conventions, industrial fairs, training sessions and all busines –related events.  We use only the most modern equipment which normally includes a cabin, speakers, head phones, microphones and necessary cables.  We can handle events for up to 2,000 people.

Consecutive interpreting is used for individuals or small groups of people who need communication in two or more languages for business meetings, plants or on-site visits, or for any other activity where verbal communication is required.
